Fast Fashion:


Inexpensive clothing produced rapidly by mass-market retailers in response to the latest trends.


Sustainable Fast Fashion:


Sustainable fast fashion focuses on finding ways to make fashion production and consumption more sustainable and socially responsible, by addressing traditional fast fashion issues like:

  • Excessive resource consumption
  • Pollution
  • Unethical labor practices
  • A throwaway culture
